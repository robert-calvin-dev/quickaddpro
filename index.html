What is Quick Add?

Quick Add is your streamlined, no-bloat, lightning-fast way to create WooCommerce products—without opening the full WordPress product editor.

How does Quick Add work?

It replaces WooCommerce's slow, multi-tab product creation with a single, AJAX-powered form that lets you: Create simple, external, grouped, or variable products Upload images directly in the form Add attributes, prices, SKUs, and variations Schedule sales Skip all the WooCommerce screen-hopping Submit everything directly to the WooCommerce product table in seconds
