What is Quick Add?

Quick Add is your streamlined, no-bloat, lightning-fast way to create WooCommerce products—without opening the full WordPress product editor.

Start Creating Products Instantly

How does Quick Add work?

It replaces WooCommerce’s slow, multi-tab product creation with a single, AJAX-powered form that lets you create simple, external, grouped, or variable products, upload images, set prices, schedule sales, and submit everything to your store instantly.
