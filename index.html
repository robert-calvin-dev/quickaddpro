What is Quick Add?

Quick Add is your streamlined, no-bloat, lightning-fast way to create WooCommerce products—without opening the full WordPress product editor.

How does Quick Add work?

It replaces WooCommerce’s slow, multi-tab product creation with a single, AJAX-powered form that lets you create simple, external, grouped, or variable products, upload images, set prices, schedule sales, and submit everything to your store instantly.

Can I add SEO metadata while creating products? (Asked by 19 people)

Absolutely. Quick Add Pro lets you input SEO titles, meta descriptions, focus keywords, and Open Graph (OG) data during the creation process — no need to edit products later.

Does Quick Add Pro support all WooCommerce product types? (Asked by 27 people)

Yes! You can create Simple, Variable, Grouped, and External/Affiliate products easily and intuitively.

How does Quick Add Pro help with bulk editing? (Asked by 33 people)

Quick Add Pro provides a clean bulk editor to adjust prices, SEO data, inventory, and more without opening each product individually — saving you hours.

Is there a limit to how many products I can add at once? (Asked by 16 people)

No hard limit. Performance depends on your hosting server. For best results, batch uploads into groups of 50–100 products at a time.

Yes! Set sale prices with start and end dates directly from the Quick Add screen, no extra steps required.

Nothing is published until you hit “Submit.” You can freely edit or delete product blocks before finalizing your upload.

Yes. Every product you create through Quick Add Pro includes proper WooCommerce Product Schema to enhance your search engine visibility.
