What is Quick Add?

Quick Add is your streamlined, no-bloat, lightning-fast way to create WooCommerce products—without opening the full WordPress product editor.

How does Quick Add work?

It replaces WooCommerce’s slow, multi-tab product creation with a single, AJAX-powered form that lets you create simple, external, grouped, or variable products, upload images, set prices, schedule sales, and submit everything to your store instantly.

Who is Quick Add for?

Store owners who add products daily and want speed. Freelancers setting up demo stores or client catalogs. Agencies launching stores that need quick prototyping. Devs tired of clicking through WooCommerce 10x per product.

Why Choose Quick Add?

While others drown you in bloated menus, Quick Add does one thing better than anyone: create WooCommerce products instantly — with built-in SEO that just works.
